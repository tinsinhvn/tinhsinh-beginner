## 📖 3. Sách

### Single-cell best practices e-book
- **Tác giả:** Heumos, L. et al.
- **Năm xuất bản:** 2023
- **Nhà xuất bản:** Nature Reviews Genetics
- **Mô tả:** This book aims to guide both beginners and experienced professionals in best practices of single-cell sequencing analysis. It provides a comprehensive overview of essential analytical steps, from preprocessing to visualization and statistical evaluation and beyond. By following this book, you will acquire the skills to independently analyze unimodal and multimodal single-cell sequencing data.
- **Link:** [E-book](https://www.sc-best-practices.org/preamble.html)
